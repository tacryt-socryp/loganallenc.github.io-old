What I've Been Up To

2014



Autoscope is a hack created at MHacks that allows a person to view their life in third person. It was implemented using a parrot drone that streams video to a Rift while the person wearing the Rift is tracked and followed using optical flow tracking.

 

Autoscope was written entirely in WebGL and other open web technologies, and won the Best Under 20 Prize presented by the Thiel Foundation.



I'm currently co-founding Slingput, a tool for creating beautiful landing pages for gathering feedback and collecting payment. I developed Slingput in Ruby on Rails. Slingput recently ranked among the top five new startups on the front page of ProductHunt.



I'm currently working as a front-end development intern at Gather. I've rewritten Gather's private events management web app for restaurants in AngularJS and created an automated testing suite using Protractor.

 

I reduced the size of the codebase for the booking platform by almost 3mb, and optimized the filtering algorithm to make it approximately 20x faster. I also helped design the new marketing site and improved SEO.



I interned at TripLingo as a software engineer and used JavaScript and Appcelerator Titanium to reimagine their translation app for use in an emergency rooms translation context. I delved into the user experience of doctors and patients, and created a streamlined app for iPads.



I wrote a calendar timeline app called Docket that utilizes canvas and integrates with Google Calendar. Docket has almost 200 downloads, and was featured in Access North Georgia and the Gainesville Times.



2013



I worked alongside Rushir Parikh to found Clerk, a news app that helps support writers through micro-donations. Our startup was mentored and advised by Pascal Finette, the past Chair of the Board of Mozilla, and Charlie Harding, a program manager at Google.



I developed a medical app for BestFit Mobile as an independent contractor, acting as the lead front-end developer on an app called ReferMD for Fresenius Medical Care, a dialysis company with 55,000 employees.

 

I created a conference room booking app for tablets for internal use at BestFit Mobile using JavaScript and Sencha Touch.
