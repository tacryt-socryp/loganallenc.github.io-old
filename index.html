What I've Been Up To

2014


I'm working as a front-end development intern at Gather. So far, I have utilized Protractor and Karma to create an automated testing suite for the upcoming release.

 

I have written large portions of the interface in AngularJS for the new release, wrote directives for form elements, and created sorting and filtering functionality for lists of bookings.

 

I created a web scraper using PhantomJS that renders AngularJS pages and saves them as static files for search engines, and wrote a data importer that filters spreadsheets and inserts them into MongoDB.



I interned at TripLingo as a software engineer and used JavaScript and Appcelerator Titanium to reimagine their translation app for use in a medical translation context.



I wrote a calendar timeline app called Docket that utilizes canvas and integrates with Google Calendar. Docket has almost 200 downloads, and was featured in Access North Georgia and the Gainesville Times.



 

I volunteered to create a website for Powerful Women, a nonprofit organization created by Shanzay Kazmi and Alexis Ajluni to empower women and raise awareness on gender inequality in the United States.


2013
